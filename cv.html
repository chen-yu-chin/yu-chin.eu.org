C H E N    Y u - C h i n


Born in Kaohsiung, Taiwan. Yu-Chin draws inspiration from senses of
harmony/disharmony and association/disassociation between the self and the world.
Through her interest in and practice of bodily perception and integration of space into
the creative process, she further draws attention to how the self experiences and
perceive time and space, and how they shape our sense of physical presence and
embodiment, or vice versa.
